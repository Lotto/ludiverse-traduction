Frequently Asked Questions

How do you count words?

We count all words in the source text, including articles and prepositions. The count is based on the original document you send us.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, for projects over 10,000 words, we apply progressive discounts. Contact us for a personalized quote.

What file formats do you accept?

Word, PDF, Excel, PowerPoint, InDesign, and most common formats. For specialized formats, please contact us.

Do you maintain confidentiality of our projects?

Absolutely. We systematically sign a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) before starting any project.

Can I modify my order in progress?

Yes, within reason. Major modifications may affect the initial deadline and pricing.

Do you offer other languages?

Mainly French-English and English-French. For other languages, we work with a network of specialized translators.
